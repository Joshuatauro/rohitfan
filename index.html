About

Rohit Gurunath Sharma was born on 30th April 1987, in Nagpur, Maharashtra. He was the eldest son of his parents Gurunath Sharma and Purnima Sharma. He has a younger brother, Vishal Sharma.

Rohit spent majority of his childhood living with his grandparents and uncles as his family struggled with poverty, his family used to live in single-room house in Dombivili.

Rohit joined his first cricket camp in 1999 under the coach Dinesh Lad, with the help of his uncle's money. His coach had asked Rohit to join Swami Vivekanand International School as it had better cricket facilities and he himself was the coach over there. Rohit, coming from a not so well-off family knew he would have struggles paying the fee's, but his coach got him a scholarship, due to which Rohit didn't have to pay a single penny for four years, and was excelling in his cricket. He started off as a off-spinner who would bat a bit, his coach saw the talent that Rohit had and promoted him as an opener instead of batting at number 8, which proved to be a excellent move as Rohit scored a century on his debut as an opener in the Harris and Giles Shield school cricket tournaments.

Rohit is an Indian International player, who also captain Mumbai Indians in the IPL. He also is the vice-captain of the Indian team in limited overs cricket
